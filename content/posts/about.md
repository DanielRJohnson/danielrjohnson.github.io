# Hi there! I'm Daniel 👋

## My Background

### 🏫 🎶 🎳

I am from Shawnee, Kansas, where I lived from when I was born in March of 2000 
until I was about 19. I had a average but fun upbringing focusing on school activities and getting 
fine grades. I did band in school from 5th grade to community college, 
and I did varsity bowling through all of high school. 

### 💻 🕹️ 🥱

From an early age I was interested in computers after playing lots of video
games. I took two programming classes in high school, and I came to know that 
I enjoyed programming even with some lackluster teaching (sorry Mr. Harrelson).

### 🎓 📕 🧑‍🏫  

After high school, I continued focusing on activities, doing band while getting
prerequisites out of the way in community college. Then my life took a quick turn.

I moved to Lawrence, Kansas to start a computer science degree at the University of Kansas.
Through my first semester I learned that I would have to take school much more seriously,
so I did just that. I wasn't doing as much of my hobbies than I would have liked, but I 
excelled through college getting very good grades.

### 🎓 👨‍💻 🏢

As I am writing this (3/11/22), I am currently in the last semester of said undergraduate degree.
I had an internship last summer as well as coming this summer in which I got to meet some great people
and work on satisfying programming projects. In the Fall I am starting my masters degree in computer 
science right here at KU, and I am passionate about the direction of my current path. 

### 🚀 🔭 🔮

While this is not a complete story, I am excited to see where it goes!
